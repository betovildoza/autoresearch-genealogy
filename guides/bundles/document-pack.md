# Document Pack

Use this when you have certificates, letters, postcards, military papers, photos, or a box of inherited documents.

## Who It Is For

- You want to turn physical or scanned material into usable evidence.
- You have more documents than organized notes.
- You want the AI to summarize and extract, not invent family links.

## What To Prepare

- Clear scans or phone document captures.
- A folder organized by family line, person, or document type.
- `Data_Inventory.md` listing each batch.
- `Research_Log.md` ready for transcription and OCR notes.

## Order

1. Use `checklists/scan-your-documents.md`.
2. Read [Document Triage](../../workflows/document-triage.md).
3. Read [OCR Pipeline](../../workflows/ocr-pipeline.md).
4. Run [06 Unresolved Persons](../../prompts/06-unresolved-persons.md) if documents mention unidentified people.
5. Run [07 Timeline Gap Analysis](../../prompts/07-timeline-gap-analysis.md) after extracted facts are in the tree.
6. Run [02 Cross-Reference Audit](../../prompts/02-cross-reference-audit.md) before treating extracted facts as settled.

## Stop And Review

Stop if:

- A scan is hard to read and the transcription may be wrong.
- A document mentions a living person.
- A name appears without enough date or place context to identify the person.
- The AI is trying to infer relationships not stated in the document.

## Human Review

Use these checks before accepting changes:

- `checklists/verify-an-ai-finding.md`
- `checklists/before-you-add-an-ancestor.md`
- `review-cards/06-unresolved-persons.md`
- `review-cards/07-timeline-gap-analysis.md`
- `review-cards/02-cross-reference-audit.md`

## Expected Outputs

- A document inventory.
- Searchable transcriptions or summaries.
- A list of people, places, and dates extracted from the documents.
- Open questions for unclear names or relationships.
